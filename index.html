VISION

Saint Michael College of Caraga Envisions to be a University by 2035 and Upholds Spiritual Formation and Excellence in Teaching, Service, and Research.


MISSION

SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and learning culture that will ensure the students with excellent and rewarding learning experience that transform lives, abound spirituality, develop skills and prepare future leaders. SMCC shall engage in dynamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary researches that are publishable to advance and achieve institutional initiatives. SMCC shall commit to serve the diverse and local communities in fostering innovations through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

GOAL

Uphold Culture of Excellence in the Areas of Spiritual Formation, Instruction, Research, and Extension, thus Produce Graduates that are Globally Competent, Spiritally Embodied, and Socially Responsible.
